Who We Are

The Premier OOH Association
in Sindh

Sindh Outdoor Advertisers Association - SOAA is the only representative trade body for media owners operating in Out-of-Home Advertising in the province of Sindh. We unite media owners, advertisers, and government to foster a future-ready OOH ecosystem.

🎯 Our Vision

Elevate Sindh OOH landscape and unlock the industry's full potential through innovative, data-driven environments.

🚀 Our Mission

Unite media owners, advertisers, and government to foster a collaborative, future-ready OOH ecosystem driven by technology.

🛡️ Advocacy

A collective voice representing the industry's interests before regulators, government bodies, and policy makers.

🤝 Community

Building meaningful connections among members through events, forums, and collaborative programs.
