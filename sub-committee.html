About SOAA

The Association uniting Sindh outdoor advertising community, for a stronger, more professional OOH industry.

SOAA Sub Committees

Co-Ordination Committee

Purpose: Ensures communication and alignment among departments.

  • Strategic alignment of departments
  • Effective communication channels
  • Coordination of activities & programs
  • Conflict resolution & problem solving
  • Monitoring and evaluation
  • Policy implementation support

Chairman: Sabir Ali / Syed Faisal Hussain

Arbitration Committee

Purpose: Resolves disputes fairly and transparently.

  • Dispute resolution & hearings
  • Evidence evaluation
  • Decision making based on policies
  • Mediation and negotiation
  • Confidential record keeping
  • Legal compliance

Chairman: Hafeez Ur Rehman

Business Development Committee

Purpose: Enhances client engagement & growth.

  • Client relationship management
  • Membership growth strategies
  • Market research & insights
  • Promotions & outreach
  • Strategic partnerships
  • Feedback & improvements

Chairman: Najeeb Farooqi

IT & Social Media Committee

Purpose: Manages digital presence and technology systems.

  • Digital strategy & online presence
  • Social media management
  • IT infrastructure & security
  • Website & content management
  • Analytics & reporting
  • Member digital support

Chairman: Syed Ammar Ul Hasan Naqvi

Fabrication Committee

Purpose: Ensures quality and standards in fabrication.

  • Develop industry standards
  • Policy advocacy
  • Training & workshops
  • Industry collaboration
  • Research & innovation
  • Quality assurance

Chairman: Muhammad Zaffar

CSR Committee

Purpose: Promotes corporate social responsibility.

  • CSR strategy development
  • Community engagement
  • Sustainability initiatives
  • Awareness programs
  • Monitoring & reporting
  • Ethical compliance

Chairman: Muhammad Javaid
