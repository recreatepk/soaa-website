About SOAA

The Association uniting Sindh outdoor advertising community, for a stronger, more professional OOH industry.

Our Vision

Elevating OOH Advertising in Sindh

Our vision is to transform the Out-of-Home (OOH) advertising industry in Sindh into a modern, organized, and globally competitive ecosystem that contributes positively to urban development and public engagement. We aspire to create a future where outdoor advertising is not only a medium of brand communication but also a meaningful part of the city’s visual identity - enhancing aesthetics, improving public awareness, and supporting economic growth.


We envision an industry that operates with innovation, transparency, and collaboration, where stakeholders-from media owners and advertisers to government authorities and the public-work together seamlessly. By embracing digital transformation, sustainable practices, and international standards, we aim to unlock the full potential of OOH advertising and position Sindh as a leading hub for impactful and responsible outdoor media.

Our Mission

Uniting Industry,
Driving Growth

Our mission is to strengthen and advance the outdoor advertising industry by creating opportunities that enhance the effectiveness of brand communication while ensuring a supportive and well-regulated environment for all stakeholders.


We are committed to representing and protecting the interests of outdoor media owners by advocating for fair policies, streamlining coordination with regulatory authorities, and addressing industry challenges proactively. Through collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing, we aim to empower our members to operate efficiently and competitively.


Additionally, we strive to foster a culture of professionalism, ethical practices, and continuous improvement within the industry. By bridging the gap between brands, agencies, property owners, and the public, we aim to deliver advertising solutions that are engaging, responsible, and beneficial for society as a whole.

Our Story

Built by the Industry,
For the Industry

SOAA was established in 2005 to represent the collective interests of outdoor advertising media owners across the province of Sindh. Although OOH is a very strong and effective medium worldwide, the local market has faced numerous challenges including regulatory complexity, payment cycle issues, and lack of industry standards.

Our founding members recognized that a unified voice was essential, one that could negotiate with government authorities, set professional standards, and build the trust that brands need to invest confidently in OOH.

From traditional hoardings to digital takeovers, SOAA is shaping how messages meet people in motion, smarter, cleaner, more responsible, and deeply connected to the pulse of our cities.

Chairman's Message

"After serving this industry for 30 years, I've witnessed it rise to success and collapse to rock bottom, not once but multiple times. Poor law and order, broken payment cycles, and mismanagement have pushed many hardworking advertisers to the edge."

My vision for these two years is clear: to restore financial stability, secure smoother payment flows, and protect the working rights of every advertiser. We will rebuild client trust by creating a more organized, professional, and reliable outdoor advertising ecosystem.

"A strong industry is built on fairness, when the advertiser works freely, the client invests confidently."

General Secretary’s Message

Sindh Outdoor Advertisers Association - SOAA

As the General Secretary of the Sindh Outdoor Advertisers Association - SOAA, and with over 30 years of experience in the outdoor advertising industry, my vision is to elevate the Out-of-Home (OOH) advertising industry through innovation, sustainable growth, and rightful recognition.

  1. Promoting Innovation: We are committed to embracing emerging technologies and creative solutions that make OOH advertising more engaging, measurable, and relevant in today’s evolving marketplace.
  2. Fostering Industry Growth: Our focus is on expanding the OOH advertising sector by creating new business opportunities, encouraging investment, and strengthening collaboration among industry stakeholders.
  3. Ensuring Industry Recognition: We strive to ensure that the Out-of-Home advertising industry receives the respect and acknowledgment it truly deserves, positioning it as a vital contributor to the national economy.

A Shared Future: Together, let us build a future where OOH advertising plays a meaningful role in economic development and is recognized as a well-organized, regulated, and stable industry.

Warm regards,
Syed Sabir Ali
General Secretary
