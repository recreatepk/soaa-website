Become a member of Sindh premier OOH trade association. Grow your network, protect your rights, and shape the industry's future.

Member Benefits

Industry Representation & Advocacy

Gain a collective voice to advocate for favorable policies, regulations, and industry standards while influencing legislative decisions.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with advertisers, agencies, and stakeholders through events and forums to build strong partnerships.

Knowledge Sharing

Access research, reports, and insights. Participate in workshops to improve efficiency and adopt new technologies.

Business Growth

Attract new clients through association directories, promotions, and industry events.

Industry Resources

Get legal guidance, compliance updates, and technical support for smooth operations.

Brand Credibility

Enhance your reputation and participate in awards and recognition programs.

Marketing Campaigns

Join collaborative campaigns and increase market visibility through collective branding.

Cost Savings

Get discounted rates for events, services, and professional support.

Advertiser Benefits

Marketing Research

Access the most extensive OOH research library to understand ROI and media effectiveness.

OOH Studies & ROI

OOH competes with TV and outperforms radio, print, and digital display while boosting search ROI.

Demographic Insights

Detailed insights on Karachi, Sindh urban/rural areas, and demographics like age, gender, and population density.

National Conference & Expo

Annual event offering networking and industry insights with key decision-makers.

OOH Education

Training resources, tools, and presentations to enhance campaign planning and execution.
